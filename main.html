What is synchronization? adjustment of rhythms of oscillators due to their weak interaction
What is an oscillator? E.g., electronic (Collpits etc., lasers), mechanical (clocks, pendulum); biology (fireflys ...). All have a few things in common: active; robust to IC; characterized by period $T$. Van der Pol = canonical model. (Toy problem?). Dyncamial system. Autonomous, self-sustained, self/auto-oscillatory [The main universal property is that these are all active systems that taken apart, or being isolated, continue to oscillate in their own rhythms.]
